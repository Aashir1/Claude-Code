# Savory Oats aur Egg Bowl

## Recipe Type
**Category:** Breakfast
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 10 minutes
**Total Time:** 15 minutes
**Servings:** 1 person

---

## Ingredients (Fresh aur Local)

### Main Ingredients:
- **Oats:** 1/2 cup rolled oats (local farm se fresh)
- **Eggs:** 2 medium size fresh eggs
- **Doodh ya Paani:** 1 cup (doodh zyada creamy banata hai)

### Vegetables (Fresh aur Local):
- **Tamatar:** 1 medium, chopped
- **Pyaaz:** 1/4 cup, finely chopped
- **Hara dhania:** 2 tablespoon, chopped
- **Hari mirch:** 1 small, chopped (optional)

### Seasonings:
- **Namak:** 1/2 teaspoon (ya taste ke mutabiq)
- **Kaali mirch:** 1/4 teaspoon
- **Haldi:** 1/4 teaspoon
- **Olive oil ya butter:** 1 tablespoon

## Instructions

### Step 1: Oats Prepare Karein
1. Ek small pot mein 1 cup paani ya doodh boil karein
2. Oats add karein aur 3-4 minutes cook karein medium heat pe
3. Namak, kaali mirch, aur haldi add karein
4. Jab oats soft ho jayein aur creamy texture aa jaye, side pe rakh dein

### Step 2: Vegetables Saute Karein
1. Ek pan mein olive oil ya butter garam karein
2. Pyaaz add karein aur golden hone tak cook karein (2 minutes)
3. Tamatar aur hari mirch add karein
4. 2-3 minutes cook karein jab tak tamatar soft na ho jayein

### Step 3: Eggs Add Karein
1. Vegetables ke saath 2 eggs crack karein
2. Eggs ko fork se lightly beat kar dein pan mein
3. Scrambled style banayein ya sunny side up
4. 2-3 minutes cook karein

### Step 4: Assemble Karein
1. Ek bowl mein cooked oats dalein
2. Upar se egg aur vegetable mixture dalein
3. Fresh hara dhania se garnish karein
4. Thoda extra kaali mirch sprinkle kar sakte hain

## Variations

### Mediterranean Style:
- Feta cheese add karein (small cubes)
- Cherry tomatoes aur olives use karein
- Fresh basil se garnish karein

### Desi Style:
- Zeera (cumin) aur garam masala add karein
- Green chutney side mein serve karein
- Fresh naan ke saath khayein

### Extra Protein Boost:
- 3 eggs use karein instead of 2
- Greek yogurt side mein serve karein
